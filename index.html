AI System for Detecting Damaged Roads

Final Project for the Artificial Intelligence Course

About the Project

We developed a computer vision system that automatically determines whether a road is in good condition or needs repairs. This solution can be integrated into smart city cameras for quick detection of road damages and prompt response.
